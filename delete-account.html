Billxpress account deletion

Delete Your Billxpress Account

You can request deletion of your Billxpress account and associated personal data from inside the app or by contacting our support team. We will review and process eligible requests within a reasonable time.

Last updated: June 13, 2026

Delete Your Account Inside the App

If you can still access your Billxpress account, please use the app to submit your deletion request:

  1. Open the Billxpress app and sign in to your account.
  2. Go to your Profile or Account screen.
  3. Open Settings, Account Settings, or Security Settings.
  4. Select Delete Account, Close Account, or Account Deletion Request.
  5. Read the notice shown in the app and confirm your request.

After confirmation, we may contact you through your registered email address or phone number if we need to verify the request or resolve an open transaction, dispute, wallet balance, or security issue.

Request Deletion by Email

If you cannot access the app, send an email to billxpress1@gmail.com with the subject line "Account Deletion Request".

Please include:

  • your registered phone number or email address;
  • your full name, if available on your Billxpress profile;
  • a short statement asking us to delete your Billxpress account.

For your security, do not include your password, full card details, OTP codes, or other sensitive credentials in the email.

What Will Be Deleted

When your request is approved, we will delete or anonymize personal information that is no longer needed to provide services, including account profile details and other personal data connected to your Billxpress account where deletion is permitted.

Information We May Retain

Some transaction records may be retained where required for security, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, accounting, audit, tax, payment processing, or legal and regulatory purposes. This may include records of wallet funding, airtime and data purchases, electricity payments, TV subscriptions, eSIM orders, bulk SMS activity, card-related records, transaction references, timestamps, and support history.

Retained records will be kept only for as long as necessary for those purposes and will remain protected according to our Privacy Policy.

Processing Time

We will review and process your request within a reasonable time after receiving the required account details. If there are pending transactions, unresolved disputes, security checks, or legal obligations, completion may take longer.
